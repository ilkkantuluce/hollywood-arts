About the Hollywood Art School


Hollywood Arts is a one-of-a-kind educational facility for homeless and former foster care young adults located in the heart of Hollywood. Classes emphasize hands-on learning, mentor relationships, teamwork, and technical skills. The Center has fully equiped computer lab, recording studio and classroom space. Classes include fashion, acting, writing and music. All classes are free and lead to work-based mentorships, internship and entry-level work opportunities.
